OUTRUN V1 – BILLING, SUBSCRIPTIONS, TEAMS & ENTERPRISE
________________________________________
OBJECTIVE
Billing should never feel like a payment page.
It should feel like an investment in business growth.
Every interaction should reinforce the value users receive.
The pricing system must be transparent.
No hidden fees.
No confusing usage.
No deceptive upgrade tactics.
Users should always understand:
What they have.
What they've used.
What they'll get by upgrading.

SUCCESS METRIC
Billing succeeds when users upgrade because they genuinely see increasing value—not because they are pressured.
The Unlimited plan should feel aspirational.
Businesses should think:
"We're growing enough that Outrun has become essential to how we operate."
That perception is the foundation of sustainable recurring revenue.
