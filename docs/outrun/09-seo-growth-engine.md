OUTRUN V1 – SEO GROWTH ENGINE (SIGNATURE FEATURE)
________________________________________
OBJECTIVE
The SEO Growth Engine is not an SEO audit tool.
It is an AI SEO Growth Consultant.
Its purpose is to help businesses generate more organic traffic, improve search visibility, and convert visitors into customers.
The engine must explain SEO in plain English.
Never overwhelm users with technical jargon.
Every recommendation should answer:
Why does this matter?
How much could it help?
How difficult is it?
What should I do next?
SEO should feel approachable to business owners.
